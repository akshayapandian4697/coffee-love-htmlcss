We're serving you the world's BEST coffee with LOVE

Medium-dark roasted coffee beans and advanced double-filter technology. A rich, full flavour in every mug.

Over the years, Coffee Love is single origin Canadian coffee. It is handled with the utmost care by our family and the families that help us work to produce the highest quality grade exportation coffee. We have been certified 100% Organic and Fair-Trade. We are working closely with indigenous-Colombian communities in the region to help us better understand the land, planting, harvesting, as well as processing the coffee to green beans with the smallest eco-footprint. This attention to detail gives Maderas Coffee an unparalleled quality and desirability.
